1. Overview

Response Resources serve as the centralized content vault for your WhatsApp communication strategy. This module allows you to create, store, and manage reusable assets—ranging from simple text snippets to complex multi-step form flows—ensuring your team delivers consistent, professional, and rapid responses to every customer inquiry.

Reusable Intelligence
Once created, these materials can be manually inserted into chats by agents or automatically triggered via Keyword Triggers for 24/7 autonomous engagement.

2. Dashboard Infrastructure

The Response Resources interface is organized by content categories to facilitate rapid retrieval and management.

Navigational Pillars
  • Content Types Panel: A vertical sidebar to filter by asset class (Text, Image, Flow, etc.).
  • Global Search: Instantly locate specific snippets or documents across all categories.
  • Material List View: A clean, searchable grid of all saved reusable content.

Pro Tip: Use descriptive naming conventions for your materials (e.g., "Welcome_New_Users") to help agents find the right content in seconds during live chats.

3. Content Types

Explore the diverse range of messaging formats supported by Wapi to create engaging and effective customer interactions.

Text Messages

Create and manage quick text snippets for faster responses. Ideal for FAQs, greetings, and simplified administrative updates. Text messages support basic formatting and can be reused across multiple automation triggers.

Images

Upload and manage image files for quick sharing. Supports JPG, PNG, and GIF formats. Visual content significantly improves engagement and helps in demonstrating products or providing visual proof in support chats.

Documents

Upload and manage documents like PDF, Word, and Excel. Share brochures, invoices, guides, and other official documentation directly through WhatsApp with ease.

Videos

Upload and manage video files for quick sharing. Perfect for product demos, tutorials, or promotional content that requires more than just a static image.

Stickers

Upload and manage sticker files for quick sharing. Add a personalized and informal touch to your customer interactions to build better brand relationships.

Form Flow

Create and manage multi-step interactive forms. Collect user information, survey responses, or service requests through a structured conversational interface.

Message Flows

Create and manage automated message sequences for customer engagement. Design complex logic paths that respond dynamically to user inputs for efficient automation.

Message Templates

Create and manage automated message templates for customer engagement. Use Meta-approved templates for proactive outbound messaging and notifications.

Product Catalogs

Manage your product catalogues within Wapi. Showcase your products and services directly to users on WhatsApp, allowing for easier e-commerce integration.

Chatbot

Manage your AI chatbots. Configure intelligent automated assistants that can handle common customer queries without the need for human intervention.

4. Provisioning Protocol

  • Step 1: Classification - Select the appropriate category from the left Content Types panel.
  • Step 2: Initialization - Click the + Add [Type] button (e.g., + Add Text Messages).
  • Step 3: Content Authoring - Enter a unique Name and the actual Message Body or upload the required file.
  • Step 4: Persistence - Click Create to finalize and store the material in your library.

5. Operational Synergy

Keyword-Driven Automation
Response Resources achieve their full potential when linked to Keyword Actions. For example, assigning the keyword "Order" to an Image material of your product catalog ensures users receive it instantly upon inquiry.
Workflow Example
  1. Create a Text Material: "Welcome to our store!"
  2. Upload an Image: "Current_Promotions.png".
  3. Map both to the keyword "OFFER".
  4. User sends "OFFER" → System sends both materials automatically.
Strategic Use Cases
  • Support: Send "Quick Troubleshoot" documents instantly.
  • Sales: Push Catalogues or Form Flows for lead capture.
  • Retention: Trigger Sequences for post-purchase follow-ups.

6. Strategic Governance

Health & Maintenance
  • Concise Messaging: Keep quick-replies short to prevent UI clutter in WhatsApp.
  • Active Review: Periodically delete outdated materials to keep the library lean.
  • Test First: Always send a test message to a sandbox account before linking to production keywords.

Troubleshooting: If a material isn't triggering via a keyword, ensure the keyword mapping matches exactly and that the material hasn't been deleted or archived.
